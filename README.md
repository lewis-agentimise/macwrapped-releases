## What's on your card

- ⏱️ **Minutes on screen** — plus days active, busiest day and peak hour
- 🏆 **Top apps, ranked** — which apps actually own your month
- 🌊 **Your 24-hour rhythm** — early bird or up at 2am, the chart doesn't lie
- 🦉 **Your Mac personality** — Night Owl, Maker, Creator, Communicator, Deep Worker, Switch Hitter, Marathoner or All-Rounder. Exactly one is yours.
- 📈 **Top X% of Mac users** — an on-device estimate of how hard you go
- 🎨 **Eight colourways** (plus a fully custom one) — Sky, Indigo, Mint, Sand, Blush, Lavender, Lemon, Midnight

## Privacy, the whole point

**Everything is computed on your Mac. Nothing is ever uploaded.** No account, no analytics, no telemetry — the only network request is the update check that points at this repo. The stats come from the usage history macOS already keeps locally; nothing you type or click is ever monitored. Details in the [privacy policy](https://macwrapped.app/privacy-policy).

## Install

1. [Download the DMG](https://macwrapped.app/download/MacWrapped.dmg) and drag **MacWrapped** to Applications.
2. Open it from the menu bar and grant **Full Disk Access** (that's how it reads the usage history your Mac already keeps — the app walks you through it).
3. Meet your month. Pick a colourway. Share the card.

Requires macOS 13 Ventura or later · Signed with a Developer ID and notarized by Apple · Updates arrive automatically in-app.
